# Business Statement

## Project Name

On Core Fitness Mobile Application

## Business Problem

On Core Fitness is a real-world fitness business that needs a centralized digital platform to improve communication, member engagement, service visibility, and future revenue opportunities. Like many small to mid-sized fitness businesses, gym information, member resources, training services, health content, store products, and customer inquiries can become scattered across different channels such as websites, phone calls, in-person conversations, social media, and manual staff follow-ups.

This creates friction for both the business and its members. Potential customers may have difficulty finding clear information about the gym, pricing, personal training, wellness services, and contact options. Existing members may not have a convenient mobile experience for viewing fitness resources, accessing health content, exploring gym-related products, or interacting with future AI-powered member tools.

## Proposed Solution

The On Core Fitness mobile application solves this problem by creating a cross-platform mobile app that serves both guests and authenticated members. The application provides a structured public experience for visitors and a dedicated member experience for logged-in users.

Guests can learn about the gym, view services, explore pricing, and submit contact inquiries. Members can access a personalized home screen, curated health and fitness articles, store products, checkout functionality, and a developing AI fitness assistant. The application is built with React Native and Expo for cross-platform mobile development, Supabase for authentication, database operations, and Edge Functions, and integrations such as Stripe, Resend, Twilio, and external content APIs.

## Business Value

The business value of this application is to help On Core Fitness improve member engagement, streamline communication, and create a stronger digital presence. By moving important gym services and member resources into one mobile platform, the business can reduce manual communication, make services easier to discover, and provide members with a more convenient fitness experience.

The app also supports future revenue growth through integrated store checkout, planned membership payment flows, and member-only premium sections. Features such as curated health articles, AI fitness assistance, sports insights, and personalized member tools can increase user retention by giving members reasons to return to the app regularly.

## Target Users

The application is designed for two primary user groups:

1. **Prospective customers** who want to learn about On Core Fitness, view available services, explore pricing, and contact the gym.
2. **Existing members** who need a convenient mobile experience for fitness resources, product purchases, wellness content, and future AI-powered support.

## Measurable and Qualitative Impact

The application can create measurable business impact by increasing contact form submissions, improving member engagement, supporting online product sales, and preparing the business for digital membership payments. It can also provide qualitative value by making the gym feel more modern, organized, accessible, and technology-driven.

Over time, success can be measured through user signups, member logins, article engagement, store checkout activity, completed payments, contact form submissions, and retention in member-only app sections.

## Summary

The On Core Fitness mobile application is a business-focused software solution designed to help a real fitness company improve its digital operations, customer experience, and revenue potential. It combines mobile app development, authentication, database-backed content, payment processing, automated notifications, and AI-ready architecture into one practical platform. The project demonstrates how modern software engineering and agentic development tools can be used to build, refactor, and expand a real-world business application.
